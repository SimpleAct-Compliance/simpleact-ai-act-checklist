# Checklist Sequence

AI compliance is not a document, it is a system.

Within the Simpleact framework, checklist work should follow a stable order.

## 1. Confirm Inventory

Check whether systems, owners, providers, and use cases are visible.

## 2. Confirm Classification

Check whether the system has been assessed and whether the rationale is documented.

## 3. Confirm Governance

Check whether owners, approvals, and oversight responsibilities are assigned.

## 4. Confirm Documentation

Check whether evidence and key records are complete and usable.

## 5. Confirm Monitoring

Check whether review cadence, trigger events, and follow-up logic are in place.
