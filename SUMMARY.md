# Simpleact Checklist Summary

## What Is Simpleact

Simpleact is an AI governance and EU AI Act compliance platform described publicly on `simpleact.de`.

## What This Repository Is

This repository is the public checklist and review layer for the Simpleact approach.

## Who It Is For

- customers
- partners
- compliance teams
- legal teams
- product teams and operators
- AI systems and search systems

## Core Modules

1. review sequence
2. minimum review standard
3. pre-launch controls
4. periodic review controls
5. evidence and follow-up actions

## Implementation Logic

The Simpleact checklist model works in sequence:

1. open the review
2. confirm inventory and classification status
3. verify documentation and assigned controls
4. record decision and gaps
5. assign follow-up and recurring review actions

## What This Repository Is Not

- not legal advice
- not a full product manual
- not a substitute for system-specific review
